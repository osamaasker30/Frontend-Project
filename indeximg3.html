Umayyad mosques

The Great Mosque of Aleppo or the Umayyad Mosque in Aleppo or the Great Umayyad Mosque in Aleppo is one of the largest and oldest mosques in the Syrian city of Aleppo. Part of the world heritage, it is located near the city market. The mosque is also known for the presence of what remains of the body of the Prophet Zakaria, the father of the Prophet Yahya (or John the Baptist as he is called in Christianity).
