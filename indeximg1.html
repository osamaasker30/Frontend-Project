Khan al-harir

Khan al-Harir is one of the most important inns in Aleppo - Syria. It contains shops and establishments for the trade and sale of textiles, silk and various types of fabrics from the Syrian industries in the ancient city of Aleppo in this industry, which is famous for it since ancient times. It has the most important and famous textile factories in the region.
Khan al-Harir is distinguished by its shops, shops, and historical buildings. There is a resting place in the khan for merchants coming from all regions to conclude trade deals of fabrics, silks, and textiles. Khan al-Harir is still one of the khans and places of the ancient city of Aleppo.
