Dederiyeh cave

The Dederiyeh Cave is a prehistoric cave located in the Samaan mountain range in Afrin, Aleppo Governorate. Excavations began in 1987 and in 1993 a skeleton of the first Dederiyeh child who lived there between 100 thousand and 40 thousand years ago was discovered. His burial shows a ritual burial ritual for the Neanderthals His burial dates back to the Middle Paleolithic period. Three important civilizations succeeded in the cave, which are the Yabrudi civilization, the Mositrian civilization, and the Natufian civilization
