Ain Dara

It is a breathtaking historical cliff located on the road connecting Aleppo to Azaz and Afrin. The city dates back to the Aramaic era between 1200-740 BC. The city currently consists of ruins and a temple that includes various statues representing winged animals, statues of the Sphinx, many inscriptions and monuments, and a basalt stela representing the goddess Ishtar was found at the site.
